Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Burma Brands collects, uses, protects, and shares information when customers and verified brands use the Burma Brands mobile or web application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information needed to provide shopping, ordering, and brand management services, including:

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and profile information.
  • Customer delivery addresses used to process orders.
  • Brand profile details, business information, product listings, stock, images, and social links.
  • Order, cart, wishlist, rating, review, and revenue-related activity.
  • Messages sent between customers and brands through the app.
  • Reports submitted from chat or product detail screens.
  • Technical information such as device, browser, app activity, and network-related data.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to create accounts, process orders, show products and shop profiles, support customer-brand communication, review reports, improve app performance, and provide customer support.

4. Payments and Refunds

Payment is currently managed directly by each brand, not by Burma Brands inside the app. Customers and brands should communicate clearly about payment confirmation, cancellations, and refunds.

5. Sharing Information

Customer order and contact details may be visible to the brand handling that order. Brand product and shop information may be visible to customers. We may also share information when required by law, to protect users, or to investigate misuse of the service.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect user information, but no online service can guarantee complete security. Users should keep account credentials private and contact Burma Brands if they notice suspicious activity.

7. User Choices

Users may update profile details, manage addresses, edit brand information, adjust product stock, and contact Burma Brands for account or privacy-related questions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, business practices, or legal requirements change. The updated document will show the latest revision date.
