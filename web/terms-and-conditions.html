2. About Burma Brands

Burma Brands is a marketplace platform that connects customers with verified Myanmar brands. Customers can browse products, contact brands, place orders, manage wishlists, and give ratings. Brands can manage shop profiles, products, inventory, customer messages, orders, and revenue views.

4. Brand and Vendor Responsibilities

5. Orders, Cancellations, and Refunds

Customers may cancel orders while they are pending. After an order is confirmed, customers should contact the brand directly. Brands may cancel orders when payment is suspicious, when customers request cancellation, or when an order cannot be fulfilled. Refunds should be handled clearly between the customer and the related brand.

6. Payments

Payment is currently managed directly by brands and not by an in-app Burma Brands payment system. Because of this, products from multiple brands may need to be ordered separately.

7. Product Upload Limits and Availability

Brands may upload up to 50 products for now. Products that have already been ordered by customers may not be deletable. Brands should set stock to 0 or leave the product until stock reaches 0 when the product is no longer available.

8. Ratings, Reviews, and Reports

Customers may rate products after orders arrive. Users should provide honest ratings and must not post abusive, false, violent, or harmful content. Burma Brands may review reports and take action when content or behavior violates these terms.

Burma Brands may verify brands before allowing vendor access. A brand that wants to stop selling should stop uploading new products, update stock as needed, and contact the Burma Brands Team for support.
